About Me

Aqeel Younas

I am Aqeel Younas, a Master’s graduate in Project Management and Business Engineering from CNAM Paris. My research lies at the intersection of entrepreneurship and strategy, focusing on how entrepreneurial leadership shapes firm competitiveness and firm performance in manufacturing SMEs. In my master’s thesis, I analyzed survey data from 200 manufacturing SMEs using PLS SEM in SmartPLS to examine both direct effects and mediation mechanisms. I am now preparing to pursue a PhD to deepen this research and contribute to evidence based knowledge on SME growth and competitiveness in emerging economies.
